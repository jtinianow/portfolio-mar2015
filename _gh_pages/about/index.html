About Jeremy

Jeremy Tinianow is a cross-disciplinary designer with a focus on bridging the gap between digital and physical. Currently a student at Boulder Digital Works, Jeremy is on an ambiguous path with a specific goal: to explore how technology informs design, and vice versa.
